Annual, creeping, 1-2 m tall flowers. The flowers are bright, very decorative, and have a pleasant smell. It grows well in a sunny place, protected from the wind, in rotting, 30 cm deep dug and loosened soil. Before sowing, it is recommended to germinate the seeds and sow them in moist soil. It is sown in nests, 3-5 seeds each, near the supports. They can be used to form a blooming and fragrant wall for one summer, picked for small bouquets.
